--- title: "Brick Arch" description: "A demo of software for generating brick arch drawings for use in diagrams and templates" layout: "project" id: "brick-arch" date: 2024-10-14 ---

While working at H.G. Matthews, a brickyard in Southeast England, I was tasked with solving the lengthy process of creating architectural drawings of brick arches for use in previews for customers and templates printed on plastic sheets for use in the manufacturing of brick arches. On this page, I have created a rudimentary demonstration of this functionality, and some of the planned features surpass even the capabilities of the original CAD program - check the to-do list to see and track my progress.
